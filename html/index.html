About Me

Designing with purpose and passion

I'm a passionate UI/UX Designer with a strong focus on creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. With a background in graphic design and front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I bridge the gap between design and development to bring ideas to life. I specialize in crafting wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity interfaces using tools like Figma.

My approach is driven by UX research, ensuring that each design decision is backed by user insights and usability principles. I've worked on diverse projects from educational platforms and e-commerce sites to mobile apps enhancing both aesthetics and functionality. I thrive in collaborative environments and am always eager to learn and grow in this ever-evolving design landscape
